Financial Literacy Basics

Dive deep into the world of personal finance with our comprehensive Financial Literacy Basics course. This program is designed to equip you with essential knowledge and practical skills for managing your money effectively, building savings, and making informed financial decisions. Through interactive lessons and real-world exercises, you'll learn about budgeting, debt management, investment fundamentals, and planning for your financial future.

Course Overview:

  • Duration: 3 semesters (1 Academic year)
  • Mode: Online, Self-paced
  • Level: Beginner-friendly
  • Certification: Certificate of Completion

Course Fee Structure
